organic choco

Most Pacari chocolate is made from Arriba Naciona, which are some of the best cacao beans available. No surprise then that they've won numerous awards. Products are innovative and the flavours interesting. Founded in 2002 by Santiago Peralta and Carla Barboto, Pacari is a business built on sustainability and quality. The name Pacari means “nature” in the Quechua, the local indigenous language, and it's at the heart of everything Pacari do.
